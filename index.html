Frequently Asked Questions

Some frequently asked questions has been answered for you.

  • KEGOW Agent
  • KEGOW Pos
  • KEGOW Wallet
  • KEGOW Banking
How do I become a KEGOW Agent?

Download the KEGOW App on Google Play Store and follow the step-by-step instructions.

What do I need to complete my registration?

Passport photograph, BVN, address and business type.

Do I need to be registered with CAC before I can become a KEGOW Agent?

No, you can become a KEGOW Agent without CAC registration.

What is next after registering with KEGOW?

After you register as a KEGOW agent, you can render the services that KEGOW offers.

What do I need to own a corporate wallet as an SME?

A business name, a business registration document, board resolution, business owner's ID and BVN.

How do I get a Pos as an agent?

You must fulfil a Level 3 KYC and pay a token of ₦20,000.

What should I do when I have an issue with failed transactions?

Report immediately to the KEGOW support team via email, call or chat.

How long does it take to resolve a reversal issue?

Reversal takes less than 10 mins to resolve.

What happens when I have failed transactions with Wallet transfers?

Report immediately to the KEGOW support team via email, call or chat.

What happens if I transfer money to the wrong account number?

Unfortunately, that is outside of KEGOW's control.

What should I do when fund is transferred from my wallet, but the receiver does not get an alert?

Alerts can be delayed, depending on the network provider. We advise that receivers should log in to their app to check their balance.

What happens when I transfer funds from my wallet and the receiver does not get the money after 24 hours?

Kindly advise the customer to be patient and report to the KEGOW support team for necessary action.
